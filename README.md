# Transika: A Monika After Story Submod
A submod for Monika After Story that makes your Monika transgender, female to male!

![I hope you enjoy!](/mokintro.png)

## What this submod does
-  Replace instances of "Monika" with your nickname for him
-  Overwrites topics to change pronouns
-  Occasionally he will mention being a trans man
-  Adds new dialogue for some things to make sense (e.g. why he wears dresses)

## What this submod does NOT do
- Change submod dialogue. If submods are installed that refer to your Transika as female, sorry I can't fix that
- Change his appearance (beard asset in screenshot can be found [here (part of a pack)](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/19x0_R_k6vPFsWR1Riudo55zdV2Fqe9VQ), hair can be found [here (part of a cosplay)](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1xTMc3aREBw1-DSApbOnK6HfxpK-Kdkto)

## What this submod plans to do in the future
- Add new topics
- Possibly add spritepacks

## Known errors
- Will refer to him as "Monika" in games
- Will refer to him as "Monika" in poems
